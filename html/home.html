We are a research team from Software Analysis Lab led by Prof. Hakjoo Oh at Korea University. This page is for introducing our research on automated software repair. In this project, we aim to develop state-of-the-art software repair techniques that are fully automated, general (i.e., not limited in fixing only certain types of vulnerabilities), reliable, and scalable. All our research projects will be open-sourced to facilitate future researches from the community. For more details, please refer to Project page.


This research project is supported by Institute of Information & communications Technology Planning & Evaluation (IITP) grant funded by the Korea government(MSIT) (No.2020-0-01337, (SW STAR LAB) Research on Highly-Practical Automated Software Repair).
